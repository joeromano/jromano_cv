\section{Ph.D. Thesis}
\cvline{Title}{\emph{Combining Tactile and Kinesthetic Information for
Improvements in Human and Machine Haptic Systems}}
\cvline{Committee}{Katherine Kuchenbecker, Vijay Kumar, Mark Yim, and Seungmoon Choi}
\cvline{Description}{\small Tactile cues provide a wealth of information and have the potential to revolutionize haptic display technology and the performance of autonomous robotics. However, this information only becomes meaningful when correlated with the motion that generated it. My thesis shows that tightly coupling sensing and acting makes it possible to engineer systems with unique advanced capabilities.}
