\section{Ph.D. Thesis}
\cvline{Title}{\emph{Combining Tactile and Kinesthetic Information for
Improvements in Human and Machine Haptic Systems}}
\cvline{Committee}{Dr. Katherine J. Kuchenbecker (advisor), Dr. Vijay Kumar, Dr. Mark Yim, and Dr. Seungmoon Choi}
\cvline{Description}{\small Tactile cues provide a wealth of information and have the potential to revolutionize both haptic display technology and the performance of autonomous robotics. However, this information only becomes meaningful when correlated with the motion that generated it. In my thesis I show that by tightly coupling sensing and acting, it is possible to engineer systems with unique and sophisticated capabilities.}
